Getting Around & Where to Stay

Cape Town offers a range of transport options, from buses and taxis to rental cars. Top neighborhoods for visitors include the City Bowl, Sea Point, and Camps Bay.

Local Foods & Restaurants

  • Try the iconic "bobotie" (spiced meat casserole)
  • Sample fresh seafood at the V&A Waterfront
  • Taste local wines in nearby vineyards
