Welcome to Cape Town

Welcome to Cape Town, the jewel of South Africa and one of the world's most beautiful cities. Nestled dramatically between the iconic Table Mountain and the sparkling waters of the Atlantic Ocean, Cape Town offers a perfect blend of natural wonders, rich history, and vibrant culture. From pristine beaches and lush vineyards to colorful neighborhoods and world-class dining, this coastal paradise has something for every traveler. Whether you're seeking adventure in the great outdoors, delving into fascinating history, or simply relaxing in breathtaking surroundings, Cape Town invites you to discover its many treasures.

Quick Facts

  • Located at the southwestern tip of South Africa
  • Population: approximately 5.1 million
  • Home to the iconic Table Mountain, one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature
  • Historic Robben Island where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned
  • Boasts over 20 Blue Flag beaches
  • South Africa's oldest city, established in 1652
